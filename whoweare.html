About Us 

 Striving for a decent life for everyone.

Welcome

ReDo means "Research and Development Organization"  which is a Tanzania based national Organization registered on 12th August 2021 with registration number 00NGO/R/2072 as a Non - Governmental Organization under the Non - Governmental Organizations Act, No. 24 of 2002 as amended from time to time. 
ReDO was formed to address the development challenges of poor access to clean and safe water, hygiene and sanitation, little awareness on human rights and gender inequality, prevalence of HIV/AIDS, communicable and non-communicable diseases, with limited youth friendly environment in sexual reproductive health and rights, upcoming challenge of climate changes with non-resilient community with little understanding of climate issues, environment and natural resources.
ReDO was formed by the team of five (5), members with vast experience and passion to address socio-economic challenges facing Tanzanian, Currently, the organization has more than 10 members who have been registered so far considerably with the organization membership policy. In the community works, team identified the above development challenges with limited evidence in addressing them which require a joint efforts;
ReDO will therefore empower community to solve their own development challenges through research and interventions leading to improved quality and affordable services in the community.

Structure

ReDO is a member-based non-government organization made up and governed by constitutionally established organs or bodies. The organization is categorized their organs in two categories;

One, is a Governing Body comprising a General Meeting and a Board of Directors.

Second, is a Management body which includes organization staff led by the Executive Director.

The Governing body leads the managing body which in turn implements the day-to-day activities of the organization and reports to the governing body.
